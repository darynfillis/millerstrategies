Bio

Steve helps clients understand House relationships, political context, and member dynamics that can shape the outcome.

Steve Ruppel helps clients understand the people, relationships, and political dynamics that shape outcomes in Congress. Clients may know an issue belongs on the Hill but still need to understand which relationships, member priorities, and political dynamics actually matter.

He identifies the members, staff, political validators, and relationship paths that can influence the issue. Access only creates value when it is tied to the right office, the right message, and the right moment.

Steve served as Political Director for Speaker McCarthy’s political operation. In that role, he traveled extensively with Speaker McCarthy, engaged with candidates who are now members, and built relationships with incumbents, chiefs of staff, and the broader McCarthy and NRCC political teams.

That experience gives clients a practical view of how congressional relationships, political timing, and member priorities interact.
