Bio

Katie helps support executive coordination, scheduling, and principal-level execution.

Katie Peil supports the CEO and helps coordinate executive priorities, scheduling, and firm operations. Senior-level advocacy requires disciplined preparation, scheduling, and follow-through.

Her work allows principals to stay focused on client strategy and execution.

Before joining Miller Strategies, Katie served as a Senior Consultant at Booz Allen Hamilton, supporting Air Force leaders on organizational strategy, change management, and event coordination. She also led technology accelerator programs at FedTech, helping entrepreneurs commercialize federal technologies and helping small businesses pitch dual-use innovations to the Department of Defense and commercial partners.

She also has Washington experience through internships at the U.S. Department of Agriculture and the White House press and communications offices. She holds a Bachelor of Science in Agribusiness and Agricultural Communications from the University of Arkansas.
